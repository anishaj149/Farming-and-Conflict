The underground fires that have burned at the World Trade Center site for the past three months have finally been extinguished, Gov. George Pataki said Wednesday.
"Just in the last week the fires have actually been put out," Pataki told a group of about 50 elected officials during a tour of the scene.
Fire officials confirmed that the fires are now considered extinguished.
