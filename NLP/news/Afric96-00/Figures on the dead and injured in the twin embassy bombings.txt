

Figures on the dead and injured in the twin embassy bombings

August 10, 1998, Monday, AM cycle

Section: International News
Length: 60 words
Body




Figures as of Monday for the dead and injured in the bombings of the U.S. Embassies in Kenya and Tanzania, according to Kenya's Department of Relief and Rehabilitation and the U.S. Embassy in Tanzania.
 
 KENYA:
Total Dead: 192
Americans: 12
Injured: 4,877
Hospitalized: 542
Treated and discharged: 4,257
 
 TANZANIA:
Total dead: 10
Americans: 0
Injured: 74
Hospitalized: 6
