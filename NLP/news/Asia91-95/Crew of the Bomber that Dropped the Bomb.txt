Body




The crew of the Enola Gay, the B-29 that dropped the atomic bomb on Hiroshima:
Lt. Col. Paul W. Tibbets, pilot, commander of the 509th Composite Group.
Capt. William S. "Deke" Parsons, weaponeer.
Capt. Theodore "Dutch" van Kirk, navigator.
Maj. Thomas Ferebee, bombardier.
Capt. Robert Lewis, co-pilot.
1st Lt. Jacob Beser, radar officer.
2nd Lt. Morris R. Jeppson, electronics officer.
Tech Sgt. George "Bob" Caron, gunner.
Staff Sgt. Wyatt Duzenbury, engineer.
Sgt. Robert Shumard, assistant engineer.
Sgt. Joe Stiborik, radarman.
Pvt. Richard Nelson, radioman.
 Load-Date: July 20, 1995
