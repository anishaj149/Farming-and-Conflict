Body




Details of the government plan announced Tuesday to open Japan's public works market to foreign companies.
---
-BIDDING PROCESS: Public works projects sponsored by the central government valued at $ 7.69 million or more will be subject to open, competitive bids similar to the U.S. system. Same applies to government corporations projects worth $ 25.69 million or more.
-LOCAL GOVERNMENTS: Exempt from these rules, although the central government will encourage them to open projects to foreign companies.
-EXCLUDED: "Routine and simple" projects, design and consulting contracts valued below $ 769,500.
-TIMETABLE: Parts of the plan will take effect April 1, with others to be phased in over the following year.
