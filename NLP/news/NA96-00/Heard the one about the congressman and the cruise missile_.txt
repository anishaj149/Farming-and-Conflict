

Heard the one about the congressman and the cruise missile?
The Associated Press
February 11, 1998, Wednesday, AM cycle

Section: Washington Dateline
Length: 65 words
Dateline: WASHINGTON
Body




With the military preparing for a possible attack on Iraq, reporters asked Senate Minority Leader Tom Daschle, D-S.D., on Wednesday whether senators first wanted to visit Baghdad to talk to Iraqi leader Saddam Hussein.
"You know, you can send congressmen or cruise missiles, I suppose," Daschle said.
Asked what the difference was, Daschle answered: "The cruise missiles are the smart weapons."
