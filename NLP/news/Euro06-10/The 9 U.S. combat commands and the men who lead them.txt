Leaders of the nine U.S. combat commands:
 U.S. Northern Command, Peterson Air Force Base, Colo.: Air Force Gen. Gene Renuart
 U.S. Transportation Command, Scott Air Force Base, Ill.: Air Force Gen. Norton Schwartz
 U.S. Joint Forces Command, Norfolk, Va.: Air Force Gen. Lance Smith
 U.S. Central Command, MacDill Air Force Base, Fla.: Navy Adm. William Fallon
 U.S. Pacific Command, Camp H.M. Smith, Hawaii: Navy Adm. Timothy Keating
 U.S. Southern Command, Miami, Fla.: Navy Adm. James Stavridis
 U.S. Special Operations Command, MacDill Air Force Base, Fla.: Army Gen. Bryan Brown, who is to be replaced next month by Adm. Eric Olson
 U.S. European Command, Stuttgart, Germany: Army Gen. Bantz Craddock
 U.S. Strategic Command, Offutt Air Force Base, Neb.: Marine Corps Gen. James Cartwright, who has been tapped to be vice chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff. Cartwright's replacement has not yet been named.
