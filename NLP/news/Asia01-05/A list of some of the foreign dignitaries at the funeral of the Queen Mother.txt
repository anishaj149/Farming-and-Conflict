Some of the monarchs, heads of government and other foreign leaders and dignitaries at the funeral in London of the Queen Mother Elizabeth:
- Australia: Prime Minister John Howard
- Belgium: King Albert II
- Brunei: The Sultan of Brunei
- Canada: Prime Minister Jean Chretien
- Denmark: Queen Margrethe II, Prince Henrik, Prince Gustav
- Jordan: Princess Muna
- Latvia: President Vaira Vike-Freiberga
- Liechtenstein: Prince Hans-Adam II
- Luxembourg: Grand Duke Henri, Grand Duchess Maria Teresa
- Monaco: Prince Albert
- The Netherlands: Queen Beatrix
- New Zealand: Prime Minister Helen Clark
- Norway: King Harald V, Queen Sonja
- Spain: King Juan Carlos, Queen Sofia
- Sweden: King Carl XVI Gustaf and Queen Silvia
- United States: first lady Laura Bush
