The 34 leaders participating in the Summit of the Americas in Quebec:
-Antigua and Barbuda: Prime Minister Lester Bryant Bird.
-Argentina: President Fernando de la Rua.
-Bahamas: Prime Minister Hubert Ingraham.
-Barbados: Prime Minister Owen Arthur.
-Belize: Prime Minister Said Musa.
-Bolivia: President Hugo Banzer Suarez.
-Brazil: President Fernando Henrique Cardoso.
-Canada: Prime Minister Jean Chretien.
-Chile: President Ricardo Lagos.
-Colombia: President Andres Pastrana.
-Costa Rica: President Miguel Angel Rodriguez.
-Dominica: Prime Minister Pierre Charles.
-Dominican Republic: President Hipolito Mejia.
-Ecuador: President Gustavo Noboa.
-El Salvador: President Francisco Flores Perez.
-Grenada: Prime Minister Keith Mitchell.
-Guatemala: President Alfonso Portillo.
-Guyana: President Bharrat Jagdeo.
-Haiti: President Jean-Bertrand Aristide.
-Honduras: President Carlos Flores Facusse.
-Jamaica: Prime Minister P.J. Patterson.
-Mexico: President Vicente Fox.
-Nicaragua: President Arnoldo Aleman.
-Panama: President Mireya Moscoso.
-Paraguay: President Luis Gonzalez Macchi.
-Peru: Foreign Minister Javier Perez de Cuellar.
-St. Kitts and Nevis: Prime Minister Denzil Douglas.
-St. Lucia: Prime Minister Kenny Anthony.
-St. Vincent and the Grenadines: Ralph Gonsalves.
-Suriname: President Ronald Venetiaan.
-Trinidad and Tobago: Prime Minister Basdeo Panday.
-United States: President George W. Bush.
-Uruguay: President Jorge Batlle Ibanez.
-Venezuela: President Hugo Chavez.
